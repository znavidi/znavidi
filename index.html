Research Experience

I have two main research experiences

Identification of Cranial Mesenchymal Stem Cells Soluble Factors Regulating Retinal Pigmented Epithelium Cells Development

2017-2018

We did bioinformatics analysis on mesenchymal cells originated from different parts of the body (head vs. trunk) to identify potential effective factors to enhance Retinal Pigment Epithelium (RPE) cell differentiation from Embryonic Stem Cell (ESC).

cell-free DNA Analysis for Mutation Detection

2018-2019

We are working on mutation detection and analysis of cell-free DNA data of breast cancer patients, and the application of deep neural networks to detect somatic mutations in cfDNA.
